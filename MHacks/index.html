Futur: Changing the World

Imagine having access to real-time social trends, personal preferences and cultural normalities everywhere you journey. Futur is combining consumer and market data with machine learning to provide an in-depth analysis on the lifestyle and preferences of individuals in almost every city across the globe! This large influx of information is globalizing our world by eliminating many barriers perceived by individuals in a diverse range of societies. Users are now able to learn more about each other while embracing a multitude of cultures!
